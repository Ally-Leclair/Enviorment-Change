What is causing climate change and why should you care?

Climate change effects animals, agriculture and the human population. Over time climate change will reshape the face of our planet and while some amount of climate change is natural, there's a more than 95 percent probability that human activities over the past 50 years have warmed our planet.

Your Carbon Footprint

Everyone makes an imapct on this planet, including you! Your Carbon Footprint is the amount of carbon dioxide and other carbon compounds emitted by a particular person, group, etc. Calculating your carbon footprint is a way to measure the environmental impact your lifestyle has. There are a number of online calculators that can help you figure out your carbon footprint. Look for some in our Learn More section
