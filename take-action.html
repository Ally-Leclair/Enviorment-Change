Steps you can take to help

My Pledge

As years pass, the climate coninues to be impacted for the worse by humans. Here are some steps that you can individually take to help create a global impact. Everyone plays a role to take care of our planet so why not start now!


Organizations to Donate to

Here are a few charities and organizations that help fight climate change. Check out the Learn More section to help in specific areas.

  • Team Trees: Donates money to help plant trees around the world
  • Sierra Club Foundation: Has many goals and plans to protect the environment
  • Environmental Defense Fund: A U.S non-profit where you can donate

Tasks

Here are some simple tasks to help our planet! Remember every tasks counts!

  • Call or write to your Senator or legislator to support environmental bills
  • Support sustainablilty brands
  • Practice greener habits like recycling
  • Calculate your Carbon Footprint
  • Optional: Donate to some of the organizations featured here
